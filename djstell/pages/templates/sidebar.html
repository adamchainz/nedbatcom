Stop SOPA

This site may contain material that infringes on someone else’s copyright, not on purpose, but it might.

The U.S. House’s Stop Online Piracy Act and the Senate’s PROTECT-IP Act would permit the government to require access be blocked to sites accused of providing infringing material, all without due process or recourse.

In solidarity with other sites like Wikipedia that are opposed to SOPA, this site is dark today.
